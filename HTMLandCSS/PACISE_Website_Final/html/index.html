Technology Empowerment - Bridging the Gaps

At this year's PACISE (Pennsylvania Association of Computer and Information Science Educators) the theme is Technology Empowerment - Bridging the Gaps.

We will highlight the importance of diversity in the field of technology and how we may empower new computer information science majors as they enter the field. There are many activities taking place during the event, so make sure to check them out. And visit the contact page with any questions.
