Bike the River Tour

The Bike the Mountains Tour rises from the town of Clarion, Pennsylvania and explores Cook Forest.Our tour follows the natural curves of the Clarion River, giving you the opportunity to bike some of the most beautiful trails in Pennsylvania. This tour is a classic showcase of Clarion's river scenery.

The Bike the Mountains Tour is amazing. I highly recommend it and would gladly return.

— Steve H.

Not designed for the weekend cyclist, this tour is offered only for those fit enough to ride high mountain passes. We provide sag wagons and support. Your lodging and meals are also part of the registration fee. We guarantee tough climbs, amazing sights, sweaty jerseys, and lots of fun.

This is the seventh year we've offered the Bike the Mountains Tour. It is our most popular tour and riders are returning again and again. Our experienced tour leaders will be there to guide, help, encourage, draft, and lead you every stroke of the way. Come join us!

Itinerary

Day 1

We start from the foothills near in Cooksburg, Pennsylvania, promptly at 9am. The first day is a chance to get your legs in shape, test your gearing, and prepare for what's to come.

Day 2

Day 2 starts with a snake trail, following the Clarion river to the Clarion River Suites. Refresh yourself at the famous suites for a leisurely dinner.

Day 3

Day 3 takes you along River Road. This 55-mile route showcases the mountains and riverfront views, providing amazing vistas from Cook's Forest State Park.

Day 4

Now for the supreme challenge: Day 4 brings some real challenging cycling through Clarington and up Millstone. It's an amazing ride with many places to observe the Pennsylvania wildlife.

Day 5

We start Day 5 in the town of Millstone and decide if you want to cross the Clarion River for an even extended ride or stay on River Road and camp at the Clarion River Campgrounds.

Day 6

On Day 6, we ride back to Clarington and spend the evening canoeing the Clarion River.

Day 7

On Day 7, we bike back to Cooksburg and then enjoy a celebratory dinner as we share memories of a great tour.
